1. COMPANY OVERVIEW
================================================================================

Nexus Technologies Inc. is a technology company focused on real-time
communication and notification infrastructure. This handbook outlines the
policies, benefits, and expectations for all Nexus employees.

  - Company Name:     Nexus Technologies Inc.
  - Founded:          2018
  - Headquarters:     Portland, Oregon
  - Satellite Office: Austin, Texas
  - Employees:        350+ (as of 2025)

Mission Statement
-----------------
"Building infrastructure that helps teams respond faster and smarter."

Our mission drives every product decision and engineering effort at Nexus.
We believe that timely, reliable communication is foundational to effective
teamwork, and our platform is designed to make that possible at scale.

Our Values
----------
  - Customer Obsession: Every feature starts with a customer problem.
  - Ownership: We take responsibility for outcomes, not just tasks.
  - Transparency: We default to open communication across teams.
  - Continuous Improvement: We measure, learn, and iterate.

2. ONBOARDING PROCEDURES
================================================================================

Welcome to Nexus! This section outlines what you can expect during your first
30 days. Your hiring manager and assigned buddy will help guide you through
each step.

Required Documents (Due Before or On Day 1)
--------------------------------------------
Before you can begin working, the following documents must be submitted to
Human Resources:

  - Signed Non-Disclosure Agreement (NDA)
  - Completed I-9 Employment Eligibility Verification
  - Emergency Contact Form
  - Direct Deposit Information

Failure to submit these documents within your first three business days may
result in a delay of system access and payroll enrollment.

Day 1: Orientation
------------------
  - Collect equipment from IT (laptop, badge, security key).
  - Meet your hiring manager for a welcome conversation and team overview.
  - Attend the company-wide orientation session at 10:00 AM Pacific.
  - Review this handbook and submit signed acknowledgment form.

Days 2-3: Compliance and Access
-------------------------------
  - Complete all compliance training modules:
      - Security Awareness Training
      - Data Handling and Privacy Training
      - Code of Conduct Review
  - Request system access via the AccessHub portal (accesshub.nexus-platform.io).
  - Standard access includes email, chat, wiki, and issue tracker.

Day 5: Integration
------------------
  - Your manager will assign an onboarding buddy from your team.
  - Schedule and hold your first 1:1 meeting with your manager.
  - Set up your local development environment using the team wiki guide.
  - Clone relevant repositories and verify build processes work end-to-end.

Day 10: Deeper Access
---------------------
  - Observe your first team retrospective meeting.
  - Access to production systems is granted after a security review conducted
    by the Platform Engineering team. Your manager must submit the request,
    and approval typically takes 1-2 business days.
  - Review the incident response runbook for your team.

Day 30: Onboarding Review
-------------------------
  - Conduct a formal onboarding review with your manager.
  - Complete the onboarding feedback survey (sent by HR via email).
  - Confirm that all system access requests have been fulfilled and are
    functioning correctly.
  - Discuss initial goals and expectations for the next quarter.

3. LEAVE POLICY
================================================================================

Nexus offers competitive leave benefits to support work-life balance. All leave
policies apply to full-time employees. Part-time employees receive pro-rated
benefits.

Paid Time Off (PTO)
--------------------
PTO is accrued monthly and can be used for vacation, personal time, or any
non-medical absence.

  Years of Service       Annual PTO Days
  --------------------   ----------------
  1 - 3 years            20 days
  4 - 7 years            25 days
  8+ years               30 days

  - Accrual: Monthly (1/12 of annual allotment credited each pay period).
  - Rollover: Unused days roll over up to a maximum of 5 days per year.
  - Rollover days must be used by March 31 of the following year.

Sick Leave
----------
  - Allowance: 10 days per year.
  - Rollover: No rollover. Unused sick days are forfeited at year-end.
  - Documentation: No doctor's note required for absences of fewer than 3
    consecutive days. For absences of 3 or more consecutive days, a medical
    certification may be requested by HR.

Parental Leave
--------------
  - Primary Caregiver: 16 weeks, fully paid.
  - Secondary Caregiver: 6 weeks, fully paid.
  - Eligibility: Available to all full-time employees after 6 months of
    continuous employment.
  - Notice: Employees should notify HR and their manager at least 30 days
    before the expected start date when reasonably possible.

Bereavement Leave
-----------------
  - Immediate Family (spouse, child, parent, sibling): 5 days.
  - Extended Family (grandparent, grandchild, in-law): 3 days.
  - Additional unpaid leave may be arranged by speaking with HR.

Leave Request and Approval
---------------------------
  - All PTO requests are submitted through the HR portal (hr.nexus-platform.io).
  - Requests for 1-2 consecutive days are auto-approved if submitted at least
    48 hours in advance, subject to team capacity constraints.
  - Requests for 3 or more consecutive days require manager approval.
  - Manager approval should be received within 3 business days of the request.
  - In cases of emergency or unplanned absence, notify your manager and HR
    as soon as practicable.

4. BENEFITS
================================================================================

Nexus provides a comprehensive benefits package designed to support employees'
health, financial security, and professional growth.

Health Insurance
----------------
Two health plan options are available through Blue Cross Blue Shield:

  Plan          Employee        Deductible     Coinsurance
  ----------    ------------    ----------     -----------
  Basic         $250/month      $1,500         80/20
  Premium       $450/month      $500           90/10

  - Both plans cover preventive care at 100% with no deductible.
  - Open enrollment occurs annually in November.
  - Changes outside of open enrollment require a qualifying life event.

Dental and Vision
-----------------
  - Dental and vision coverage is included in both health plans at no
    additional cost to the employee.
  - Dental covers two cleanings per year, basic restorative procedures,
    and orthodontia (50% co-pay, $2,000 lifetime maximum).
  - Vision covers an annual eye exam and one pair of frames or contacts
    per year.

401(k) Retirement Plan
----------------------
  - Company Match: Nexus matches up to 4% of your salary.
  - Vesting Schedule: 25% per year of service (fully vested at 4 years).
  - Investment Options: A range of index funds and target-date funds are
    available through our plan administrator.
  - Enrollment: Employees may enroll at any time after their start date.

Professional Development
------------------------
  - Annual Budget: $2,000 per employee for conferences, courses, and
    certifications.
  - Pre-Approval: Required from your manager before incurring expenses.
  - Reimbursement: Submit receipts through the expense portal within 30 days.
  - Internal learning resources and lunch-and-learn sessions are available
    year-round at no cost.

Remote Work
-----------
  - Up to 3 days per week with manager approval.
  - Remote employees must be available during core hours: 10:00 AM - 3:00 PM
    Pacific Time.
  - A stable internet connection (minimum 25 Mbps) is required.
  - Remote work arrangements are reviewed quarterly.

Equipment
---------
  - Company Laptop: Refreshed every 3 years. Standard configuration includes
    16 GB RAM, 512 GB SSD, and a current-generation processor.
  - External Monitor: Provided upon request through the IT service desk.
  - Peripherals (keyboard, mouse, headset): Available upon request.
  - All company equipment must be returned upon termination of employment
    (see Code of Conduct, Section 5).

5. CODE OF CONDUCT
================================================================================

All Nexus employees are expected to act with integrity and professionalism.
The following policies are mandatory and apply to all employees, contractors,
and temporary workers.

Data Handling
-------------
  - Customer data must never leave Nexus-controlled systems.
  - Downloading customer data to personal devices is strictly prohibited.
  - Access to customer data is logged and audited on a regular basis.
  - Violations of data handling policies may result in immediate termination
    and legal action.

Equipment Return
----------------
  - All company equipment (laptop, badge, security key, monitor, peripherals)
    must be returned within 5 business days of termination or separation.
  - Equipment should be returned to IT at either the Portland or Austin office,
    or shipped via the pre-paid label provided by HR.
  - Failure to return equipment may result in the cost being deducted from
    the final paycheck, to the extent permitted by law.

Remote Work Security
--------------------
  - A secure, password-protected Wi-Fi network is required when working
    remotely.
  - The company VPN must be active whenever accessing internal systems,
    including email, chat, wiki, and production environments.
  - Employees must not use public Wi-Fi (coffee shops, airports, etc.) to
    access Nexus systems without an active VPN connection.
  - Multi-factor authentication (MFA) is required on all company accounts.

Conflict of Interest
--------------------
  - Employees must disclose any potential conflict of interest in writing to
    HR within 10 business days of becoming aware of the conflict.
  - Examples include outside employment, financial interests in competitors,
    and personal relationships with vendors or clients.
  - Failure to disclose a conflict of interest may result in disciplinary
    action up to and including termination.

Reporting Violations
--------------------
  - Nexus is committed to a culture of accountability and transparency.
  - If you witness or suspect a violation of company policy, you are encouraged
    to report it through any of the following channels:
      - Ethics Hotline: 1-800-NEXUS-ETH (available 24/7, anonymous reporting)
      - Email: ethics@nexus-platform.io
      - Direct report to your manager or any member of the HR team
  - Nexus maintains a strict non-retaliation policy. No employee will face
    adverse action for reporting a concern in good faith.
